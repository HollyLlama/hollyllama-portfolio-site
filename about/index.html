A Little About Me

My name is Holly Yánez and HollyLlama is my creative brand. I am a web developer & designer with a background in marketing.

My goal is to help individuals and companies realize the power of their own unique brand from logo design to a full-blown website.
Sometimes clients just need a little styling refresh or updates to an existing website. Other times, they need the works; new logo, new brand guidelines, new website, new social media content schedule and an official branding launch.

I can handle all of it. I specialize in simplifying the process for my clients so they can see their vision come to life without the headache of all the extra details. I love partnering with entrepreneurs, small businesses and individuals with big dreams!

I started my journey in web development and design while I was working as a marketing manager in downtown Boston. Since then, I have built hand-coded webpages for friends, family, and clients using HTML, CSS, SASS, JavaScript, jQuery and WordPress/PHP.

Through these projects I take hand-drawn wire frames and turn them into high fidelity web and mobile design files using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe XD. I design assets such as social icons, logos, print and digital collateral. I also edit images for projects using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and even Procreate.
